Spectacle Power Analyzer — v5.0

Lens Power
Wave Scanner

Point your back camera at a pattern, then wave your glasses in front — the app auto-detects each lens pass and builds an averaged reading. No buttons needed mid-scan.

🌊
Wave Mode
Just wave glasses in front of camera. App auto-detects lens-in/out cycles and averages results automatically
🎯
Auto Focus-Distance
Uses camera autofocus API (if available) to calculate power from focus shift — no distance setting needed
📡
Camera Specs
Reads focal length, sensor capabilities, focus distance range, and uses them to improve accuracy
🔬
FFT + Autocorrelation
Dual-method hybrid analysis with IQR outlier rejection across multiple wave cycles
💊
Sphere + Cylinder
Independently analyzes H and V spatial frequencies to detect astigmatism (cylindrical power + axis)
📤
Export
Download full report as JSON or CSV, or print a clinical prescription sheet
📋 What you need:
Spectacle glasses to test Phone with back camera Good even lighting Ruler (optional, improves accuracy)
